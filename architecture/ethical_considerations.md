# Ethical Considerations in Astra AI

## Overview

Astra is designed to enhance personal safety while respecting user privacy and autonomy.

---

## Privacy Protection

- location data is processed securely
- minimal data retention principles applied
- user controls data sharing

---

## User Consent

- safety mode is user-activated
- alerts are triggered transparently
- no hidden tracking

---

## Bias & Fairness

- system avoids demographic profiling
- risk scoring based on behavior, not identity

---

## False Positives

- system includes user confirmation step
- alerts are not triggered blindly

---

## Responsibility

Astra is designed to support users and communities, not replace emergency services.

---

## Ethical Goal

To create a safety system that is:

- protective
- respectful
- transparent
- user-centered
