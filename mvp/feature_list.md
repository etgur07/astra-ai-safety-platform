# Astra MVP Feature List

## Core MVP Features

### Panic Alert Button
A one-tap emergency alert that sends the user’s location to trusted contacts.

### Call 911
A direct emergency call action available during high-risk situations.

### Safety Circle
Users can add trusted contacts who receive alerts during emergencies.

### Real-Time Location Sharing
Users can temporarily share their location during activities such as walking home or meeting someone new.

### Safety Check-In System
The app prompts users to confirm they are safe after a certain period of time.

### Emergency Notification System
If the user does not respond to a safety check-in, alerts are automatically sent to the Safety Circle.

### Incoming Trusted Contact Alert
Trusted contacts receive incoming emergency alerts with live location, risk level, and response actions.

### AI Safety Assistant
Users can interact with Astra through chat and voice for safety-related support.

### AI Risk Detection
The system evaluates missed check-ins, inactivity, and behavioral signals to generate a safety risk score.

### Identity Verification
Users can verify identity through selfie or ID-based verification to improve trust and platform safety.

### Evidence Capture
The app supports:
- audio recording
- video recording
- photo capture
- automatic evidence capture
- secure cloud upload
