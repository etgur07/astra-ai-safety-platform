
# Astra MVP Feature List

## Core MVP Features

### Panic Alert Button
A one-tap emergency alert that sends the user’s location to trusted contacts.

### Safety Circle
Users can add trusted contacts (friends, family, guardians) who receive alerts during emergencies.

### Real-Time Location Sharing
Users can temporarily share their location during activities such as walking home or meeting someone new.

### Safety Check-In Timer
The app prompts users to confirm they are safe after a certain period of time.

### Emergency Notification System
If the user does not respond to a safety check-in, alerts are automatically sent to the Safety Circle.
