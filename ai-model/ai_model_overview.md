# Astra AI Risk Detection System

## Overview

The Astra AI system is designed to proactively detect potential safety risks for users based on behavioral, location, and interaction signals.

Unlike traditional safety apps that rely on manual panic triggers, Astra introduces a predictive safety layer.

---

## Objective

To identify early indicators of unsafe situations and trigger protective actions before escalation.

---

## Key Inputs

- GPS location data
- timestamp patterns
- movement speed and direction
- route deviation
- safety mode activation
- missed or delayed check-ins

---

## Core Outputs

- dynamic risk score (Low / Medium / High)
- real-time safety prompts
- automatic alert triggers to safety contacts

---

## Example Scenario

1. User activates Safety Mode
2. Astra tracks movement in real-time
3. System compares movement with expected route
4. If anomaly detected (stop, deviation, inactivity):
   → risk score increases
5. If threshold reached:
   → Astra prompts user ("Are you safe?")
6. If no response:
   → alert sent to Safety Circle

---

## Innovation

Astra shifts personal safety systems from:

Reactive → Proactive  
Manual → Intelligent  
User-triggered → AI-assisted
